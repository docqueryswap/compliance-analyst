
EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into by and between ABC Corp ("Employer") and John Doe ("Employee").

1. Compensation. Employee shall receive a salary of $5,000 per month, payable on the 1st of each month.

2. Termination. This agreement may be terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

3. Confidentiality. Employee agrees not to disclose any proprietary information during or after employment.

4. Governing Law. This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
